Jerry O'Sullivan
Ryanair's Agile Journey

Ryanair is a true Irish Success story and over the past 5 years it has made technology a central tenant in it’s future as it has grown it’s Ryanair Labs function to power the future of the airline. Hear how coordinating multiple teams in multiple locations is only possible with modern software engineering practices . The talk will give a sneak peek into Ryanair and Ryanair Labs. Learn how we deliver change at a rapid pace and how we are influencing the wider organisation to embrace agile as he make the journey to become the amazon of travel.

Jerry O'Sullivan

Digital and Innovation Programme Manager at Ryanair

Jerry is an experienced digital leader with a wide range of experiences across multiple industries. He is a passionate believer in how people and culture drive change and sees Agile as a great way to deliver change but understands how difficult implementing agile practices can be. A Cork native exiled in Dublin peoplesrespublicofcork.com is never far from the top of his browsing history.
